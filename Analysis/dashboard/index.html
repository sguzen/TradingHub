What this dashboard measures

Two complementary studies on every NQ 1-hour candle since 2014. The Breakout Study asks what happens after an hour breaks the prior hour's range — does the next hour push further (follow-through) or fade right back (reversal)? The Quarter Study asks what happens inside each hour — where do the high and low form across the four 15-minute quarters? Both studies share the same filter set in the sidebar; switch between tabs to see each study's headline metrics, the Edge tab for temporal stability and hour-of-day patterns, and the Conditional tab for cross-quarter probability tables.
