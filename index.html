CandleScience — Sweep + CISD
4 timeframe pairs · Prior candle sweep logic
  • Sweep: Prior HTF candle's high or low is violated in the Q1 window (first portion of the new candle)
  • Close-back: Price must close back inside the prior candle's range, confirming the manipulation
  • CISD: A 1M candle body closes through the open of the first opposing delivery run — confirmation trigger
  • Entry: Next candle open after CISD · Stop: Sweep extreme · Target: 2R from manipulation range

TTFM° — TTrades Fractal Model
6 T-Spot variants · HTF zone entry logic
  • C1–C2–C3: Three consecutive 1H candles. C1=context, C2=reference (swept), C3=trigger (most recent)
  • T-Spot Zone: C3.close ↔ sweep midpoint (log-weighted), activated at C3 close
  • Entry: Limit at sweep_mid when price retraces into the zone · Stop: C3 extreme
  • 6 Variants: Normal / Expansive / ProTrend × BULL / BEAR (classified by C3 sweep percentage)
