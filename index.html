Model Mechanics
🌊
Fractal Sweep — Sweep + CISD
3 timeframe pairs · Prior candle sweep logic
  • Sweep: Prior HTF candle's high or low is violated anywhere within the new candle
  • Close-back: Price closes back inside the prior candle's range, confirming the manipulation
  • CISD: The CISD candle closes through the open of the last opposing delivery run — confirmation trigger
  • Entry: Next candle open after CISD · Stop: Sweep extreme · Target: 1R (simple_1r profile)
