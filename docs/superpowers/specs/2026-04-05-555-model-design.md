# 555 Model — Design Spec

## Overview

New backtesting model in `555/` that detects setups based on three 5-minute conditions in sequence: **SMT divergence → Inverse FVG → CISD**. Forked from Fractal Sweep's architecture (data loading, outcome resolution, stats aggregation, dashboard design) but with entirely new detection logic.

**Single timeframe:** All detection on 5M. No model variants.

## Detection Pipeline

All detection operates on 5m-resampled NQ and ES bars within RTH (7:00–16:00 ET).

### Step 1: Build 5m Swing Fractals

Identify swing highs and swing lows on both NQ and ES 5m charts using simple fractals:
- **Swing high:** candle whose high > both prior and next candle's highs
- **Swing low:** candle whose low < both prior and next candle's lows

Precomputed once per instrument as arrays of `(timestamp, level, type)`.

### Step 2: Detect SMT Divergence

For each NQ 5m candle that sweeps (trades through) a prior NQ swing high/low:
- Find the temporally corresponding ES swing high/low (same session, nearest in time)
- Check if ES also swept its swing level
- **Bullish SMT (LONG):** NQ sweeps swing low, ES does NOT sweep its swing low
- **Bearish SMT (SHORT):** NQ sweeps swing high, ES does NOT sweep its swing high

### Step 3: Detect Inverse FVG (after SMT)

After SMT fires, scan forward on NQ 5m for a 3-candle FVG that gets inverted:

- **For LONG (bullish SMT):** Find a bearish FVG (candle1.high > candle3.low = gap down), then price trades back up through the gap. IFVG zone = [candle3.low, candle1.high]. The gap becomes support.
- **For SHORT (bearish SMT):** Find a bullish FVG (candle1.low < candle3.high = gap up), then price trades back down through it. IFVG zone = [candle3.high, candle1.low]. The gap becomes resistance.

**Minimum IFVG size: 2 points** (gap must be >= 2 pts to qualify).

### Step 4: CISD Confirmation (after IFVG)

After IFVG forms, scan forward for CISD using body-only delivery logic (same as Fractal Sweep):
- Backward walk from return point to find consecutive opposing delivery run
- `cisd_level` = open of the first (earliest) candle in that run
- Forward scan for close through CISD level
- Dojis (close == open) are neutral — skipped, don't break the run

### Step 5: Entry

Next 5m candle open after CISD confirms.

## Trade Management

### Stop Loss

SL = IFVG invalidation, checked on **5m candle closes only** (not intra-candle wicks):
- **LONG:** SL triggers when a 5m candle closes below the IFVG low
- **SHORT:** SL triggers when a 5m candle closes above the IFVG high

`base_risk = |entry_price - ifvg_invalidation_level|`

### Take Profit

- **TP1 at 1R:** 90% of position exits. LONG: TP1 = entry + base_risk. SHORT: TP1 = entry - base_risk.
- **Runner (10%):** After TP1 hit, stop moves to breakeven (entry price). Runner marks to market at EOD (16:00 ET) or BE stop, whichever comes first.
- `net_r = 0.90 * 1.0 + 0.10 * runner_exit_r`

### Outcome Resolution

- **SL:** Checked on 5m candle closes (unique to this model — not highs/lows)
- **TP1:** Checked on highs/lows (intra-candle, standard)
- **Runner BE stop:** Checked on highs/lows (standard)
- **EXPIRED:** Neither SL nor TP1 hit within session (16:00 ET)

### Filters

- Minimum IFVG size: 2 points
- `MAX_RISK_PTS = 112.5` (MNQ $225 / $2.00 per point)
- No minimum risk points filter

## Statistics & Output

### JSON Structure (`model_stats.json`)

Single model key `5M_SMT_IFVG_CISD` containing:

- `summary` — total trades, wins, WR, EV, PF, avg risk pts, avg RR
- `dir_summary` — LONG vs SHORT breakdown
- `by_hour` — stats grouped by entry hour (7–15)
- `by_session` — PRE / NY1 / NY2 breakdown
- `by_dow` — Monday through Friday
- `by_year` — annual performance
- `equity_curve` — cumulative R, min equity, max drawdown
- `mae_mfe` — per-trade MAE/MFE percentages + hourly-normalized variants
- `trades` — full trade list with all fields

### MAE/MFE Studies

- MAE/MFE as % of entry price
- Hour-normalized MAE/MFE (% of hourly range)
- PTQ recommendation (MFE breakeven trigger): highest reach_rate where P(positive exit | MFE >= X) >= 0.70
- Optimal SL recommendation (MAE-based): tightest MAE where P(genuine loss | MAE >= X) >= 0.70

### Profile

Single profile: `structural_dynamic` — SL at IFVG invalidation, TP1 at 1R, 90/10 split with runner.

## Constants

| Constant | Value | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| `MIN_IFVG_PTS` | 2.0 | Minimum IFVG gap size in points |
| `MAX_RISK_PTS` | 112.5 | MNQ $225 / $2.00 per point |
| `ACCOUNT_SIZE` | 4500 | $ account size |
| `RISK_PER_TRADE` | 225 | $ risk per trade |
| `POINT_VALUE` | 2.0 | $ per point (MNQ) |
| `SESSION_HRS` | (7.0, 16.0) | RTH window in ET |
| `OUTCOME_MAX_BARS` | 360 | Max 5m bars to scan for outcome (session-bounded) |
