MODEL OVERVIEW SWEEP + CISD DETECTION
The Concept
WHAT IS A
FRACTAL SWEEP?
01 / TERRITORY
Prior Candle Forms
A higher-timeframe candle closes, marking a high and a low. This becomes the territory — a key structural level the market will revisit.
Sweep TF: 4H · 1H · 30M
02 / THE BAIT
Price Sweeps the Extreme
In the next candle's open, price reaches just beyond the prior high or low — triggering stops, flushing liquidity — then closes back inside the range.
Filters: min 0.10% · max 1.50%
03 / THE STRIKE
CISD Confirms
A lower-timeframe candle — the Change in State of Delivery — confirms that price has rejected the sweep and is now moving in the opposite direction. Entry is on the open of the next bar.
CISD TF: 15M · 5M · 3M

TRADE RULES RTH 07:00 – 16:00 ET
Execution Protocol
SETUP
MECHANICS
01 Prior Candle Higher-TF candle forms the structural high or low to watch. Range must meet minimum size floor to qualify. Sweep TF bar
02 Sweep Next candle's wick pierces the extreme by 0.10%–1.50% and closes back inside the prior range. Both conditions required. 0.10% – 1.50%
03 CISD Signal Within 8 bars on the lower timeframe, a candle confirms the directional shift. No CISD within 8 bars → setup is invalid. ≤ 8 CISD bars
04 Entry & Exit Enter at the open of the candle after CISD confirmation. Stop at the sweep extreme. Target at 2× risk. Expires at 16:00 ET. 1:2 · 16:00 expire

THE HUNT DAILY EXECUTION PROTOCOL
Using It Live
HOW TO
RUN THE HUNT
01 PRE-MARKET
Mark Your Territories
  • Identify key higher-TF candles that closed the prior session
  • Mark the 4H, 1H, and 30M highs/lows on chart
  • Note which are near high-probability levels (prior day H/L, overnight range)
02 SESSION OPEN
Watch for the Sweep
  • Monitor price in the Q1 window of each bar
  • Watch for a wick pierce beyond the marked extreme
  • Candle must close back inside the range — both conditions required
03 CISD ENTRY
Confirm & Enter
  • Drop to CISD timeframe (15M / 5M / 3M) after sweep detected
  • Wait for a confirming candle in the reversal direction
  • Enter at open of the next candle — do not chase
04 RISK MANAGEMENT
Define the Risk
  • Stop at the sweep extreme (the liquidity grab point)
  • Target at 2× the stop distance (1:2 minimum)
  • Trade expires at 16:00 ET — no overnight holds
