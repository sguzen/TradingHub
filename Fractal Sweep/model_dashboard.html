Model Overview
Fractal Sweep + CISD · Setup Anatomy Sweep → Return → Confirm → Execute
Setup Conditions
1
Q1 Sweep
Within Q1 (first 8–60 min of the anchor period), price sweeps the prior anchor candle's high or low. Sweep must be 0.10%–1.50% of the prior range.
2
Return to Range
Price closes back inside the prior anchor range — confirming the sweep was a liquidity grab, not a genuine breakout.
3
CISD Confirmation
Change in State of Delivery: price closes beyond the open of the first candle of the opposing consecutive delivery leg within 8 CISD bars.
Trade Mechanics
Entry Next bar open after CISD bar closes
Stop Sweep extreme — the wick that swept the anchor level
TP1 1R — 1:1 risk-reward
Runner 2R — 1:2 risk-reward · primary backtest target
