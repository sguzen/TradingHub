Model Overview
Fractal Sweep + CISD · Setup Anatomy Click to expand · Sweep → Return → Confirm → Execute
Setup Conditions
1
Sweep
At any point within the anchor period, price sweeps the prior candle's high or low — taking out resting liquidity.
2
Return to Range
Price closes back inside the prior anchor range — confirming the sweep was a liquidity grab, not a genuine breakout.
3
CISD Confirmation
Change in State of Delivery: price closes beyond the open of the first candle of the opposing consecutive delivery leg.
Trade Mechanics
Entry Next bar open after CISD bar closes
