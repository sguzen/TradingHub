How It Works Model Rules
01
FVG Detection
Scans the 9:31–9:59 ET window each RTH session for a classic 3-candle Fair Value Gap pattern. A bullish FVG forms when C3 low > C1 high, creating an unmitigated gap above. A bearish FVG forms when C3 high < C1 low. The gap boundaries define the trade zone.
9:31–9:59 ET Window
02
Entry & Stop
Fill is confirmed when price retraces to the FVG edge (C3 boundary) on any bar after C3. The stop is placed structurally — below min(C1.low, C2.low) for longs, above max(C1.high, C2.high) for shorts. Risk distance varies per trade. Position is sized so a stop-out = exactly $225.
C3 Fill · Structural Stop
03
Exit Rules
Split-exit model. 50% of the position exits at TP1 = 0.10% (Protect the Queen level) in the trade's favour. The remaining 50% runner moves its stop to breakeven (entry price) and holds until stopped out at BE or the session closes at 16:00 ET. If price never reaches TP1, the full position exits at the structural stop.
Structural Stop · TP1 0.10% (50%) · Runner BE to EOD
