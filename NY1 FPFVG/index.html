How It Works Model Rules
01
FVG Detection
Scans the 9:31–9:59 ET window each RTH session for a classic 3-candle Fair Value Gap pattern. A bullish FVG forms when C3 low > C1 high, creating an unmitigated gap above. A bearish FVG forms when C3 high < C1 low. The gap boundaries define the trade zone.
9:31–9:59 ET Window
02
Entry & Stop
Fill is confirmed at C3 — the candle that closes the FVG. Entry is at C3 open. The stop is placed at a fixed % below/above entry (e.g. 0.04% for combo #1). Contracts are sized so a full stop-out = exactly $225. Trade triggers from C3+1 bar through 16:00 ET.
C3 Fill · Fixed % Stop
03
Exit Rules
Full position exits at the fixed % TP target from entry (WIN) or at the fixed % SL (LOSS). If neither is touched by 16:00 ET, the trade expires with R = 0. Position is sized so a stop-out costs exactly $225 (5% of $4,500 account) regardless of stop size.
